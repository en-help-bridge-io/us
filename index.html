Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Trézór Bridge® different from other cross-chain solutions? +

Trézór Bridge® integrates directly with TreZor™ hardware wallets, ensuring private keys never leave your device during cross-chain transactions. Our proprietary security protocol combines multi-party computation (MPC) with TreZor's proven secure element technology, creating an unprecedented security standard for cross-chain transfers. Unlike software-only solutions, we provide hardware-level protection throughout the entire bridging process.

Which blockchains are supported by Trézór Bridge®? +

Currently, Trézór Bridge® supports Ethereum, Bitcoin, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and Solana, with plans to add support for additional Layer 1 and Layer 2 networks quarterly. Our modular architecture allows for rapid integration of new chains while maintaining consistent security standards across all supported networks.

How are fees calculated for cross-chain transfers? +

Trézór Bridge® employs a transparent fee structure consisting of three components: network gas fees (variable based on current congestion), a nominal fixed processing fee (0.1% of transaction value), and any applicable cross-chain liquidity provider fees. All fees are clearly displayed before transaction confirmation, with no hidden charges. TreZor™ Suite™ subscribers enjoy reduced processing fees.

What security measures protect my assets during transfer? +

Every Trézór Bridge® transaction is protected by: 1) Hardware wallet verification requiring physical confirmation, 2) Time-locked smart contracts that allow cancellation of suspicious transfers, 3) Multi-signature vaults for all bridged assets, and 4) Continuous on-chain monitoring for anomalous activity. Our security architecture has undergone third-party audits by leading blockchain security firms.
